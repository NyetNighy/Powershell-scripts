Install-Module -Name ExchangeOnlineManagement
Block:
New-TenantAllowBlockListItems -ListType Sender -Block -Entries "DomainOrEmailAddress1","DomainOrEmailAddress1",..."DomainOrEmailAddressN" <-ExpirationDate Date | -NoExpiration> [-Notes <String>]
Example:
New-TenantAllowBlockListItems -ListType Sender -Block -Entries "test@badattackerdomain.com","test2@anotherattackerdomain.com" -ExpirationDate 8/20/2022


Allow:
New-TenantAllowBlockListItems -ListType Sender -Allow -Entries "DomainOrEmailAddress1","DomainOrEmailAddress1",..."DomainOrEmailAddressN" <-ExpirationDate Date | -NoExpiration> [-Notes <String>]
Example:
New-TenantAllowBlockListItems -ListType Sender -Allow -Entries "test@badattackerdomain.com","test2@anotherattackerdomain.com" -ExpirationDate 8/20/2022
