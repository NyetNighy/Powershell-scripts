Download the SharePoint Online Management Shell.

Connect-SPOService -Url https://<Domain>-admin.sharepoint.com -Credential <Admin Account>

Get-SPODeletedSite -Identity <URL>
If you don't know the URL of the deleted OneDrive, run the following command:
Get-SPODeletedSite -IncludeOnlyPersonalSite | FT url
If the OneDrive appears in the results, it can be restored.Restore the OneDrive to an active state:
Restore-SPODeletedSite -Identity <URL>Assign a site collection administrator to the OneDrive to access the needed data:
Set-SPOUser -Site <URL> -LoginName <UPNofDesiredAdmin> -IsSiteCollectionAdmin $True
